About Me

Here are a few short paragraphs about myself. I used to ride a motorcycle, I thoroughly enjoyed riding. My favorite part about riding was going through the "twisties" and "hitting" turns (leaning into/over) as far I could, to drag my pucks (a device attached to the knee and designed to be dragged against the ground). Night riding was my favorite thing to do. I had equipped my bike with numerous multi-color LED lights (legal in Maryland), and glowed like a super nova when riding on the empty highways at night.
